NDT Standard Flawed Specimens Demonstration Kit (UT, MT, PT, RT & VT)

NDT Standard Flawed Specimens Kit. (UT, MT, PT, RT & VT) For NDT Demonstration.

Total Kit: 5 specimens: 3 Plates, 1 Pipe and 1 Tee 15 Randomly Placed Discontinuities Flaws.

MT/PT (1) Tee @ 0.25”T x 4” x 8” x4”

VT (1) Plate @ 0.25”T x 4” x 8”

UT (1) Plate @ 0.625”T x 4” x 8”

RT (1) Pipe 4” Sch80 (0.337” wall) x 8” & (1) Plate @ 0.625”T x 4” x 8”

Flaws: 2 to 3 for each specimen, 15 total Flaws.

Documentation: NDT Verification Reports, C of C, CAD Drawings, RT Film, and Test Sheets.

Material: Carbon Steel. (Customized Calibration Test Block and Flaw Specimen also available. Contact - info@ndetech.org.in)
